FIND OUT; HOW YOU CAN RENT A BIKE

Book your reservation

You can book a reservation for a bike of your liking in no time. Just use the Formular to select a date, a time and the bike you wish to rent. As soon as you’re done, you have successfully reserved a bike.

Show more

Pick up your bike

After you reserved a bike you can pick up the bike at our store. You just need to scan the QR code you will receive as soon as the reservation was successful. Enjoy your ride :)

Show more

Enjoy your ride

You’ve finally picked up the bike and now you’re ready to go. Over your rental period, BIKE UP will provide all the important informations such as duration, distance or battery stats.

Show more

Return the bike

As soon as you’re done, please return the bike to one of our bike stations. This will automatically register your rental as done and end the rental period. You will receive a sales slip within the next minutes.
